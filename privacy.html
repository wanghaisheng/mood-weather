Information Collection

We collect information to provide and improve our emotional tracking services. The types of information we collect include:

  • Account Information: Email address, password, and profile details
  • Emotional Data: Your mood entries, weather metaphors, and journal content
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app, including features used and time spent
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and browser type

Important Note

All emotional data is encrypted and stored securely. You have complete control over what you share and with whom.

Data Usage

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize our services
  • Generate insights about your emotional patterns
  • Improve our weather metaphor system
  • Send important notifications and updates
  • Maintain and enhance app security

AI Analysis

Our AI systems analyze your emotional data to provide personalized insights. This analysis is automated and confidential.

Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for all emotional data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Employee training on data protection

Security Measures

We use industry-standard encryption protocols and regularly update our security practices to ensure your data remains protected.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a readable format
  • Opt-out of data processing for research

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account:

  • Personal information is deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized data may be retained for research
  • Backup data is removed within 90 days

Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party services for:

  • Cloud storage and processing
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • User authentication

Third-Party Privacy

All third-party services are contractually bound to protect your privacy and comply with data protection regulations.

Cookies Policy

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze app performance
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Age Restriction

If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete this information immediately.

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes through:

  • Email notifications
  • App notifications
  • Website announcements
